WHAT IS NEEDED FOR LEARNING ENGLISH? (OR THE SECOND LANGUAGE)

The skills framework helps us to do everything, like read aloud, swim or drive. For mastering a second language must have that 3 skills:

Listening

It needs not only to identify a word but also quickly understand the phrase. Besides, need to understand the speech from different people.  With the understanding of spelling word by word we hardly understand it in the whole sentence.

Speaking

Require to voice your thoughts on the fly. (i.e. never at a loss for words). Due to the fact of memorizing words translation, the situation when you can’t recall words can be generally, only you need is understanding the context of the word.

Writing

Visual memory effect on our writing ability. The more we read, the more graphic images of words will be remembered, the easier it is to write. Visual memory helps us to improve our spelling!
